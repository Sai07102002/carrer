About Us

DreamDev Academy, a premier software training institute, was founded in 2011 and is located in KPHB (Kukatpally), Hyderabad, Telangana. With over 13 years of excellence, the academy has built a stellar reputation for delivering high-quality training programs and facilitating successful placements. Renowned for its commitment to empowering aspiring professionals, DreamDev Academy continues to be a trusted name in software education, shaping countless careers across Hyderabad and beyond.

Mission:

At DreamDev Academy, our mission is to transform education by offering comprehensive learning solutions tailored for engineering graduates and computer science professionals. We strive to bridge the disconnect between conventional academic frameworks and the dynamic demands of the tech industry, empowering students with the skills and knowledge required to thrive in their careers.
